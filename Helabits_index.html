    Habit-Building Tips

    • Start small. Focus on one habit at a time.
    • Set realistic and measurable goals.
    • Track your progress daily.
    • Celebrate your successes!
    • Visualize your goals to stay motivated.
    • Stack habits by associating new ones with existing routines.
    • Create reminders and alerts to stay consistent.
    • Share your progress with a friend for accountability.
    • Reward yourself for achieving milestones.
    • Be patient—habit formation takes time.
    • Be humble through the journey.
    • Never give up.
    • Unmake and remake yourself!
    • Get the WIN and make yourself PROUD!
